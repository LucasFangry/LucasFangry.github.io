Rongyao Fang

I am a B.Eng. student at Department of Electronic Engineering @ Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I major in Electronic Engineering (ranked 1st).

I was a visiting scholar in MIT CSAIL, under the supervision of Prof. Dina Katabi, working with Tianhong Li and Lijie Fan. I worked on a project about 3D computer vision and wireless sensing. Previously, I worked as a research assistant in SJTU Vision and Learning Lab advised by Prof. Bingbing Ni.

My research interests lie in computer vision and deep learning, particularly their applications in wireless sensing and other related topics.

I will join University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign to pursue my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Haitham Hassanieh in fall 2020.
